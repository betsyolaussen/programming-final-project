Important Information

Ingredients

  • The following ingredients are in all of our products: baking soda, citric acid, food grade PEG, fragrance.
  • The following ingredients are in SOME of our products: cosmetic grade pigment.
  • The following ingredients are used as toppings on SOME of our products: sugar, mica.

  • Allergy warning: All of our products are made in the same facility, so traces of any and all of the above ingredients could end up in any of our products.

Warnings

Common sense warnings: Small parts. Choking hazard. Not for children under 3. Not edible. May make your tub slippery. Keep out of eyes and mouth. In rare cases, skin irritation may occur. Always test this product on a small area of skin before initial use. Do not use if your skin is cracked, damaged or sunburned.

Shipping

We generally ship products within 3-5 business days of receiving your order, using priority mail. If you do not receive your products within this timeframe, please notify us at info@dabombfizzers.com.

Please be sure to provide us with accurate shipping information when placing your order. The customer is responsible for all charges incurred when a package is considered undeliverable or returned to us by the shipper due to incorrect or incomplete shipping information.

A tracking number is provided when your order ships.

Our products are packaged in (recycled) plastic to keep moisture OUT and fragrances IN during shipping and storage. Please dispose of this packaging responsibly.

Returns

Our bath bombs are fresh and made to order so, therefore, we can not accept returns. If there is a problem with your order, or if you are unhappy for any reason, please email us at info@dabombfizzers.com and we will do our best to make it right.

FAQ's

Why are da Bomb Bath Fizzers better than other bath bombs?

Why are da Bomb Bath Fizzers better than other bath bombs?

  • They smell wonderful.
  • Every one has a surprise inside!
  • They're made by hand in the USA.
  • They're fun to use, since the baking soda and citric acid set off an impressively strong fizzing reaction when you put them in the water.
  • They contain moisturizing oils.
  • Many of our competitor's bombs are overloaded with up to 22 ingredients and thick, greasy additives. We take a more simple, balanced approach to our bath bombs. They won’t fill your bath with gunk, and our pigment colors won't stain your skin. Instead, they produce what we like to call a "clean fizz."

What’s the shelf life of da Bomb bath fizzers?

Our fizzers will stay good for a year or more if kept in a cool, dry place in their original packaging. They are always made fresh, and our fragrances are strongest at the time of production. These fragrances may slowly begin to dissipate after about 6 months. If you want to enjoy the full effect of our wonderful fragrances, we recommend using them within a few months of purchase.

My bath bombs arrived, and they each look slightly different. Why?

Each of our products is handmade. Therefore, they will each have slight differences in appearance. It’s all part of the creation process and helps set our bath bombs apart!
